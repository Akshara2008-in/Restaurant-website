About Us

Welcome to Aashiyaana Restaurant, a peaceful dining destination nestled in the heart of the hills. At Aashiyaana, we believe food is not just a meal—it’s an experience that brings comfort, joy, and togetherness.
Surrounded by beautiful mountain views and fresh air, our restaurant offers a warm and cozy atmosphere where every guest feels at home. Whether you are here for a family dinner, a romantic evening, or a quiet escape, Aashiyaana is the perfect place to relax and enjoy.
Our menu brings together authentic flavors, fresh ingredients, and carefully crafted dishes inspired by both local and multi-cuisine tastes. Every dish is prepared with love to give you a memorable dining experience.
At Aashiyaana Restaurant, we don’t just serve food—we serve comfort, happiness, and memories in every bite.
